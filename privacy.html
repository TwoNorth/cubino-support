Privacy Policy

CUBINO Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

CUBINO (“the Game”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the Game and how it is used.

Information We Collect

CUBINO does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as names, addresses, or email addresses.

The Game may locally store gameplay-related data on your device, including:

  • High scores
  • Progression data
  • Unlocked cosmetics
  • Game settings
  • Statistics and gameplay preferences

Advertising

CUBINO may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Device and usage information
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This information may be used to:

  • Provide and improve advertisements
  • Prevent fraud
  • Measure ad performance
  • Improve app stability and gameplay experience

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics and Diagnostics

The Game may collect anonymous analytics or crash diagnostic information to help improve stability, performance, and gameplay experience.

Children’s Privacy

CUBINO is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Data Deletion

Users may remove locally stored gameplay data by deleting the application from their device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
